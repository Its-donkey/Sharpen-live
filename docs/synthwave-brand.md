# synth.wave Brand Concept

## Palette (hex)
- **Night Horizon**: `#0b1024` — deep indigo base for backgrounds.
- **Neon Magenta**: `#ff2fb4` — primary accent for calls-to-action and logo glow.
- **Cyber Cyan**: `#28e7ff` — secondary accent for highlights and outlines.
- **Pulse Purple**: `#7d5bff` — surface layer for panels and cards.
- **Sunrise Amber**: `#ffb347` — warm contrast for status or hover flares.

## Gradient Example
- **"Pulse Grid"**: linear gradient 135deg from `#0b1024` → `#7d5bff` → `#ff2fb4` → `#28e7ff`.

## Logo Idea: Neon Wave Glyph
- A circular disc with an inner wave line, framed by a soft neon ring, evoking oscilloscopes and sunset silhouettes.
- Works at small sizes as a favicon and scales for hero placement.

## Typography
- **Logotype**: Bold geometric sans (e.g., Audiowide or Exo 2) with tight letter spacing.
- **Body**: Clean sans (e.g., Inter) for readability on dark backgrounds.

## Usage Notes
- Pair neon accents with ample negative space on the Night Horizon base.
- Use soft glows (8–16px spread) around magenta/cyan elements for retro synth feel.
- Keep button states: default magenta fill, cyan outline on hover, amber flash on active.
